This is a fun tribute page to my favorite band!

"Old Dominion is an American country music band formed in Nashville, Tennessee. The band consists of Matthew Ramsey (lead vocals), Trevor Rosen (guitar, keyboards), Whit Sellers (drums), Geoff Sprung (bass guitar), and Brad Tursi (guitar)."

  1. "Old Dominion was chosen because it is a nickname for the state of Virginia and four of members of the group have links to Virginia."
  2. "First No.1 song is Break Up With Him"
  3. "Their music is contemporary country with rock instrumentation and has pop overtones."
